About All the Buzz Sprinklers

Our Expertise

Marcus started working with sprinklers when he was 18, under the mentorship of an incredible man named Don. Each day after installing a sprinkler system, Marcus would accompany Don on all the service calls. They spent countless hours in the truck and on the job, with Don teaching Marcus how to work hard and quickly without taking shortcuts. Don emphasized the importance of doing the job right and maintaining good communication and integrity as a way of life.


Marcus aims to continue Don's legacy by doing the job right the first time and teaching others along the way. We hold ourselves to high standards in both our fieldwork and online services. If you need a tech and live in our service area, don't hesitate to reach out.
